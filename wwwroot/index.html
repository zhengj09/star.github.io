    Platform

    We use Amazon Web Service(AWS) for computation powers, continuous integration, automatic deployment and scheduled maintenance, and Github for source code control.

    Methodology

    We apply C++ based private library for analytics and python based automatic machine learning approach for predictive modeling, data cleaning and unsupervised learning.

    Data

    We host non-sql data in hybrid database (PostgreSQL + MongoDB + Redis Cache), and provide a uniform data access layer for streaming, batch and API access models.

    About Us

    We are self-driven and results/projects oriented full-stack analytics team with 30+ years working experience in developing quantitative models, credit analytics, asset securitization structure for major wall street firms (both sell-side/buy-side).

    Our Mission

    Build a next generation, high-speed, auto-scale exchange based end-to-end platform with security enhanced research environment full of data, and a controlled production universe for end-users. It is what we call an “enterprise innovation platform”.

    Our Plan

    Bottom up plan starts with foundamental and infrastructure then expand to business/project oriented working environment. We target to deliver intelligence datalake platform, capital exchange platform and extend financial product coverage to all securitization products.

    Our Vision

    We think Finance + Techonology + Analytics will be the future trend for all finance related business success. A platform handles all aspects will create a edge on its business growth. Our vision eyes on future and business success.


    Models & Products

    Our services provide below quantitative finance models for Asset-Back Securities (ABS).

    Rates Model

    • China Shibor (Shanghai Interbank Offered Rate) curve models for ABS indexing and discounting
    • China Government Bond curve for benchmarking

    ABS Models

    • Deals structuring, loan/note origination
    • Ecnonomic scenario assumptions
    • Macro scenario analysis
    • Structuring interactive dashboard

    Agency Rating Model

    • Agency model factors explanation
    • Internation agency model: S&P, Moody's and Fitch
    • Local agency model: Chengxin

    Cashflow Analytics

    • Risks: Discount Margin, Option Adjusted Spread(OAS), Yield-To-Maturity, Weighted Average Life and etc.
    • Cashflow Projection Model
    • Sensitivity Analysis

    ABS Structure Details

    • Deal capital structure, IC/OC triggers, Reserve accounts, Senior/Sub/Performance Fee accounts
    • Apply Interest Rate, default rate, prepayment rate change into the deals and stress scenarios
    • Waterfall visualization

    Rating Agency Model Details

    • Default and credit migration matrix model
    • Derive attachment and detachment for each rating tranche
    • What if for new deals structure?
